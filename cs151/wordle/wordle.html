Try out this simple version of Wordle!

You have 6 attempts to guess the secret word by entering 5-letter words. As you guess, you'll receive a clue under each letter.

♥ Correct letter in the correct spot
♡ Correct letter in the incorrect spot
_ Incorrect letter (not in the secret word)

If you enter something that is not a valid 5-letter word, your guess will be rejected (you'll see ".....").

Good luck!
