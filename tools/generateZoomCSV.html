Randomly generate zoom breakout rooms

Instructions:
  1. Enter your participant email addresses in the text area, separated by commas or on different lines.
    • If someone has multiple email addresses associated to them,
      use an equals sign (=) to associate them to one participant.
    Here's an example input with two participants (Mary Lyon has 3 emails associated her):
    jorge@mhc.edu,marylyon@mhc.edu=mary.lyon@alternative.com=mlyon@otherplace.com
  2. Enter the number of rooms you want to randomly distribute participants into.
  3. Click the Generate and download CSV button.
    This will download a file named breakoutrooms.csv and show the results below.
